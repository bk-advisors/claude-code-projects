## Production Notes

### Transitions
- Frame 1→2: Logo shrink is the transition (no cut needed)
- Frame 2→3: Slide-left or fade-through
- Frame 3→4: Fade or slide-up
- Frame 4→5: Quick cut or fade

### Music Guidance
Look for royalty-free tracks tagged: "corporate intro," "modern positive," "tech startup." 10–15 seconds, with a clean ending sting. Suggested sources:
- YouTube Audio Library (free)
- Epidemic Sound
- Artlist

Style: clean piano or synth pad, light percussion, builds slightly, resolves cleanly. Avoid: heavy bass drops, dubstep, overly dramatic orchestral.

### Tools for Assembly
These still frames can be assembled into a video intro using:
- **Canva** (free video editor — import frames, add transitions + music)
- **CapCut** (free — good for text animation and timing)
- **DaVinci Resolve** (free, professional — full control over keyframes)

Import each frame as a 3-second slide, add transitions between them, overlay the music track, and export at 1080p.

### Voiceover (Optional)
See `channel-intro-script.md` for an optional ~15-second narration. If used, layer it over the music starting at 0:01. If skipped, the music-only version works well on its own.
